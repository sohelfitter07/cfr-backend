Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 3, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you:

  • Request services or schedule appointments
  • Contact us via phone, email, or contact forms
  • Make payments for services
  • Subscribe to our newsletter

Types of data collected: Name, contact information, equipment details, payment information, service history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide repair and maintenance services
  • Process payments and send invoices
  • Communicate about appointments and service updates
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Equipment manufacturers for warranty claims
  • Service providers assisting our business operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for all online transactions
  • Secure storage of physical service records
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data
  • Regular security audits

5. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@canadianfitnessrepair.com

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies for:

  • Essential site functionality
  • Analytics to improve user experience
  • Marketing campaigns (you can opt-out)

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

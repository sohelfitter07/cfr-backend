Common Treadmill Problems We Fix

Treadmill Won't Start

If your treadmill isn't powering on, it could be due to a blown fuse, faulty power switch, or damaged power cord. We diagnose and repair all power issues.

Strange Noises

Squeaking, grinding or knocking sounds often indicate worn bearings, loose components, or motor issues. We identify and fix the source of the noise.

Console Errors

From blank screens to error codes, we troubleshoot and repair all console and display issues across all major brands.

Speed Inconsistency

If your treadmill speed fluctuates unexpectedly, we can replace worn drive belts, faulty motor controllers, or damaged speed sensors.

Incline Failure

We repair incline mechanisms including motor replacements, controller repairs, and sensor recalibration.

Belt Issues

From slipping belts to tracking problems, we provide professional belt alignment, tensioning, and replacement services.

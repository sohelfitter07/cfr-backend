Common Elliptical Problems We Fix

Power Issues

If your elliptical won't turn on, we diagnose power supply problems, fuse issues, and faulty wiring.

Strange Noises

Squeaking, grinding or knocking sounds often indicate worn bearings or drive system issues.

Console Errors

From blank screens to error codes, we troubleshoot and repair all console issues.

Resistance Problems

If resistance isn't working properly, we can repair motors, controllers, and sensors.

Pedal Issues

We fix sticking pedals, uneven motion, and alignment problems.

Stability Problems

Wobbly machines? We can tighten frames, replace worn parts, and restore stability.
